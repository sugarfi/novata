STATEMENT OF INTENT
 
 
The Autonomous Individuals Network (AIN) has been established to push forward into the future with practical applications of functional and demystified magick, using all techniques available to consider whether it be pagan, tribal, ceremonial, modern, or un-charted. We believe in EVERYTHING and that all things ARE possible. We form our network on the foundation of Thee Temple of Psychick Youth (NorthAmerica), and all the history and knowlege that community has gathered since its creation in the 1980's, and we set our sites to new horizons..

With a focus on the manipulation of media as magickal tool in the new Aeon, AIN acts as an blueprint or map for the INDIVIDUAL to follow for building greater personal strength, insight, and network individuality. It is a process of individual and collective experimentation and research with no finite answers, dogmas or unchallengeable truths. It is for each to discover his or her own understanding of the questions that suggest themselves, and through that voyage of discovery to find their personal and true identity, the True Will, the FOCUSED POTENTIAL.

We are an organization of INDIVIDUALS dedicated to updating and de-mystifying religious thought. A community founded as network where trust, help, and sharing are key tools for better interaction as humans. We are also dedicated to creating a world where Individuals can be free to express themselves by whatever means they wish to. We wish to break Control at all levels. We experiment with whatever methods we can utilize to accomplish these goals - magick, technology, poetry, musick, whatever!

As first steps towards change, we attempt to cultivate an awareness of the consequences of our thoughts and actions, and to direct our energies in constructive directions. All this is done on the understanding that our thoughts and behaviour form the interface between our lives and the lives of others, and their repercussions are therefore endlessly returning.

Awareness is consequently a requirement for our personal and collective survival and evolution. Still, we recognise that awareness itself is dependent on information, communication, and personal commitment to ACTIVE involvment with The Network. Our work is subsequently practical and well...WORK!

Exchanging theories, models, and methods we have found useful to ourselves, we form a bond with others within this Network. There is EFFORT required to being ACTIVE. Thus we do not dictate, but rather focus on expanding the available possibilities through thee cross-fertilisation of suggestions, successes, and failures, and the promotion and propagation of our collective creative notions through multiple outlets simultaneously. And for us this is a full-time commitment, a continual process of being, an endless myriad of becomings, an ever expanding canvas on which to paint life upon.
