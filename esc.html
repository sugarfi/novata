when you open a website you make assumptions.

you assume it will look a certain way and allow you to do certain things and respond in a certain way to those things. 

        what if a website didn't do those things?

of course, one can't do too much - the web has restrictions, and for good reason we don't want to allow this sort of behavior

        but what is allowed is enough to at least make someone a bit disconcerted, hopefully

and sometimes we are reminded of all the power a simple webpage has
